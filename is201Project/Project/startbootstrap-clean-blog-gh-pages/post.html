Utah Innovation Fund

Sep 2023 - Apr 2024

While interning at Utah Innovation fund I was able to learn the fundamentals of Venture Capital. After just a few weeks interning at the firm I was able to create a deal memo on the funds now portfolio company, Rebel Medicine. Creating the deal memo helped me see full picture what goes into venture capital due diligence. Once completed both me and another analyst were able to pitch the fund's investment committee. The deal was moved forward, and we closed on a $250,000 investment.
