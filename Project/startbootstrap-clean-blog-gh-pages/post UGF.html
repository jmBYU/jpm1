University Growth Fund

Oct 2023 - Dec 2023

University Growth Fund's Venture Capital Case Competition helped me learn how to work with teams. When I first signed up for the competition I didn't know anyone else that wanted to compete. I was paired with two strangers and got one of my co-workers to join us. We all quickly became friends throught the hard work we completed. Through Utah Innovation Fund I was able to leverage a deal and use it for the competition. Through countless hours we all came togehter to complete a structured memo. I dove in and learned all I could about the product and how it worked. Others worked on understanding the management team, financials, and market size. After completing the memo we were able to present our thesis to a panel of real venture capitalist. We were judged on our performance and managed to place 2nd in state.
