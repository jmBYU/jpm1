What Do Venture Capitalist Look For In A Start-up?

  1. Venture capitalist look for are great founders
    • Someone who dreams of something crazy, and can make it a reality
    • Many venture capitalist like there to be multiple founders with varying skill sets
  2. Venture capitalist look for potential to be monopolies
    • These companies might have some sort of patent or propritory data
    • They are one of the first movers to a market and quickly take most of the market share
    • The companies have a moat to protect them against future incumbents
